Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Data Intelligence Lab at POSTECH, advised by Prof. Hwanjo Yu. My research focuses on developing robust deep learning models that can effectively handle data imperfections encountered across diverse data modalities, including time series, molecular graphs, network graphs, images, and natural language. I aim to advance knowledge-integrated learning that reliably overcomes imperfect data by bridging AI methodologies with domain expertise to tackle real-world challenges in science, medicine, and industry. With extensive experience in both academic and industrial projects, I strive to connect AI innovation with real-world industrial applications and sustainability.

Research Interest

  • Robust Learning under Noisy and Imperfect Data
  • Graph Neural Networks for Molecular and Biomedical Data
  • AI-driven Process Optimization and Smart Plant
