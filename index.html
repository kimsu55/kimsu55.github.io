Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Data Intelligence Lab at POSTECH, advised by Prof. Hwanjo Yu. My research focuses on developing robust and interpretable AI models that integrate domain knowledge from chemical engineering and biomedical applications. I have extensive experience in both academic and industrial projects, bridging AI with real-world process optimization and sustainability.

Research Interest

  • Robust Learning under Noisy and Imperfect Data
  • Graph Neural Networks for Molecular and Biomedical Data
  • AI-driven Process Optimization and Digital Twins
